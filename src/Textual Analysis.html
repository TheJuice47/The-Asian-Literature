A. Themes

  1. Theme: Refugee Experience and Displacement - The story captures the harrowing journey of Vietnamese refugees fleeing their homeland in search of safety and a better future.
  2. Theme: Survival and Resilience - The narrative highlights the physical and emotional struggles of those escaping war, emphasizing their determination to survive against overwhelming odds.
  3. Theme: Identity and Loss - The refugees face the challenge of holding onto their cultural identity while adapting to an uncertain future, mourning the lives and homes they left behind.

B. Language and Style

  1. Word Choice - The story employs vivid, often haunting descriptions to convey the harsh realities of the journey, creating an immersive and emotional experience for the reader.
  2. Sentence Structure - The narrative shifts between fragmented thoughts, urgent descriptions, and reflective passages, mirroring the chaos and trauma of the refugee experience.
  3. Tone and Overall Style - The tone is intense and poignant, balancing fear, hope, and desperation in a way that deeply resonates with the reader.
