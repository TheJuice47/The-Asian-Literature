C. Characterization

Main Characters

  1. Mai - The story employs vivid, often haunting descriptions to convey the harsh realities of the journey, creating an immersive and emotional experience for the reader.
  2. Mai's Family - Her relatives, each representing different aspects of the refugee struggle, from fear to resilience.
  3. Other Refugees - Fellow travelers who add depth to the narrative, showcasing the collective experience of displacement.

Character Development

Mai transforms from a fearful child into a more aware and resilient individual as she witnesses suffering and loss firsthand.

Motivations and Conflicts

The primary motivation for all characters is survival, while the internal conflict lies in grappling with fear, grief, and uncertainty about the future.
